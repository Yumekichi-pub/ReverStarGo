## Long Description

### What is ReverStarGo?

ReverStarGo is an original strategy board game that blends two timeless classics -- Reversi and Go -- into something entirely new. Played on a distinctive six-pointed star hexagonal board, it introduces the CP (Core Point) capture rule inspired by Go, adding a layer of territorial strategy on top of Reversi's familiar flipping mechanic. The result is a game that is easy to pick up but deeply rewarding to master.

### Features

- **Original hexagonal board** -- A six-pointed star layout creates a fresh strategic landscape unlike any traditional board game.
- **Reversi + Go hybrid rules** -- Flip your opponent's pieces like Reversi, but watch out for the CP (Core Point) capture rule drawn from Go.
- **7 AI difficulty levels** -- From Beginner to FINAL, there is always a tougher opponent waiting.
- **Daily challenges** -- A new puzzle every day. Collect monthly trophies by completing them all.
- **Rank-up system** -- Progress through 12 ranks: Leather, Bronze, Iron, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Emerald, Ruby, Sapphire, Obsidian, Master, and Diamond.
- **Free to play, no ads** -- Completely free with no advertisements or in-app purchases.
- **Play anywhere** -- Works in any modern browser. Install as a PWA on mobile or desktop for an app-like experience.
- **No account required** -- Jump in and start playing immediately.

### How to Play

Place your pieces on the hexagonal board to flip your opponent's pieces to your color, just like Reversi. But be careful -- if all the Core Points (CPs) around a group of your pieces are occupied by your opponent, that entire group is captured and removed, just like in Go. Balance offense and defense to outscore the AI.
